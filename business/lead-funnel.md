# 0xAudit — Lead Generation Funnel

## Stage 1: Top of Funnel (Awareness)

**Goal:** Attract attention, establish expertise, generate inbound interest.

### Channels & Tactics

| Channel | Tactic | Frequency |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| Twitter/X | Security tips, vulnerability threads, memes | Daily |
| LinkedIn | Professional posts, case study summaries | 3x/week |
| Moltbook | AI agent community engagement | 2x/week |
| GitHub | Open-source security tools & scripts | Monthly |
| Medium/Mirror | Long-form write-ups of real findings | Bi-weekly |

### Content Types
- **Twitter Threads:** "We found X critical vulns in Y days — here's how"
- **Free Mini-Audits:** Automated quick scan (headers, SSL, CORS) — captures email
- **Security Tips:** "Top 5 smart contract mistakes in 2025"
- **Breaking News Commentary:** React to major hacks within hours
- **Infographics:** Vulnerability statistics, before/after security posture

### Lead Capture
- Free scan landing page → email capture
- Newsletter signup ("Weekly Web3 Security Digest")
- Twitter DM automation for engaged followers

---

## Stage 2: Middle of Funnel (Consideration)

**Goal:** Build trust, demonstrate capability, nurture leads.

### Content & Assets

| Asset | Purpose | Delivery |
|-------|---------|----------|
| Case Studies | Prove ROI of audits | Website + LinkedIn |
| Sample Reports | Show report quality | Gated PDF download |
| Webinars | "Securing Your DeFi Protocol in 2025" | Monthly, live + recorded |
| Comparison Guides | "Manual vs Automated Audits" | Blog + email drip |
| Testimonials | Social proof | Website + social |

### Email Drip Sequence
1. **Day 0:** Welcome + free security checklist
2. **Day 3:** Case study — "How we saved $2M for a DeFi protocol"
3. **Day 7:** Common vulnerabilities guide
4. **Day 14:** Webinar invitation
5. **Day 21:** Limited-time trial audit offer

### Engagement Signals (Lead Scoring)
- Downloaded sample report → +20 pts
- Attended webinar → +30 pts
- Visited pricing page → +40 pts
- Replied to email → +25 pts
- Score ≥ 80 → move to Bottom of Funnel

---

## Stage 3: Bottom of Funnel (Decision)

**Goal:** Convert qualified leads into paying clients.

### Conversion Tactics

| Tactic | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Free Consultation Call** | 30-min scope discussion, threat landscape overview |
| **Trial Audit** | Quick automated scan with summary findings (free or $99) |
| **Custom Proposal** | Tailored scope, timeline, pricing within 24h |
| **Urgency Triggers** | "Post-hack" outreach, compliance deadlines |
| **Social Proof** | "X protocols audited, $YM in assets secured" |

### Sales Process
1. Lead books consultation → calendar link (Calendly)
2. 30-min discovery call → understand scope, timeline, budget
3. Custom proposal sent within 24 hours
4. Follow-up at Day 2 and Day 5
5. Contract signing → onboarding within 48h

### Objection Handling
- **"Too expensive"** → ROI calculator: cost of audit vs cost of exploit
- **"We use automated tools"** → Show what automated misses (real examples)
- **"We'll do it later"** → Stats on exploits per month, recent hacks
- **"Never heard of you"** → Case studies, Moltbook reviews, GitHub presence

## Stage 4: Retention & Expansion

**Goal:** Keep clients, upsell, generate referrals.

### Retention Program

| Activity | Frequency | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Monthly Security Checkup | Monthly | Catch new vulns from updates |
| Quarterly Threat Brief | Quarterly | Industry-specific threat intel |
| Priority Retesting | On-demand | Verify fixes after patches |
| Dedicated Slack Channel | Always-on | Quick security questions |

### Upsell Paths
- Basic → Pro: "Your app grew, your attack surface grew too"
- Pro → Enterprise: "Continuous monitoring catches what point-in-time misses"
- Any → AI Agent Audit: "Your AI agents have APIs too"

### Referral Program
- 15% commission on referred client's first engagement
- Mutual case study publication
- "Security Partner" badge for referring organizations
